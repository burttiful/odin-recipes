Pan-Fried Lemon-Pepper Tilapia

Description

If you like fish but don't like spending your month's grocery bill on expesnive, wild-caught salmon or the like, look no further than the mighty tilapia for your sustenance!

This recipe involves using a simple lemon-pepper seasoning to have a tasty meal with only minor preparation needed. Ready in 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Tilapia Fillets (Thawed)
  • Lemon-Pepper Seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat a frying pan to medium heat.
  2. While pan is heating, take a paper towel and pat-dry any fillets you will be cooking. This will help avoid the splatter of oil and butter when adding fish to the pan.
  3. Coat bottom of pain in butter by going over it once with a piece in hand.
  4. Gently rub tilapia fillets in the lemon-pepper seasoning immediately before adding to frying pan.
  5. Cook for a few minutes on each side, flipping when able to see the now-white, cooked section, esp. on the sides of the fish.
  6. Optional: Steam a side of vegetables or cook a small portion of rice to eat as a side-dish.
  7. Bon appetit!
