Tortilla Pizza

Description

This is an easy, 5- or 10-minute recipe to make some fun, homemade pizza for you and yours!

Perfect for a lazy meal for a date night in, an activity for the kids, or a camp meal.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray/Oil
  • Flour Tortillas
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Desired Toppings (e.g. pepperoni)

Steps

  1. Preheat a frying pan to medium heat.
  2. Spray bottom of pan with a small amount of cooking spray or oil.
  3. Place a single tortilla in the pan. You should feel the bottom of the tortilla sizzle as you set it in the pan.
  4. Quickly add the tomato sauce by spooning it onto the tortilla -- enough to leave a thin layer of sauce across the entire surface.
  5. Add enough shredded cheese to cover the sauce, more or less.
  6. Add desired toppings in desired quantities.
  7. Optional: Add more cheese on top of toppings. This will help to hold the toppings in place while cutting pizza slices and eating, but is not entirely necessary.
  8. When cheese is melted, remove from pan. Let cool for approximately 5 minutes before eating. Bon appetit!
