Kodiak Cakes

Description

There isn't a healthier (but still delicious) alternative to the classic panakes-for-breakfast. Even your kids will love them!

Kodiak cakes are packed with vital protein, made with 100% whole grains, and come in various flavors, including cinnamon oat, dark chocolate, peanut butter, and buttermilk.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray/Oil
  • Kodiak Cakes Flapjack and Waffle Mix
  • Water
  • Optional: Milk to replace water
  • Optional: Eggs, if using milk instead of water

Steps

  1. Preheat a frying pan to medium heat.
  2. While pan is heating, mix one cup of Kodiak Cakes Flapjack and Waffle Mix and 1 cup of either water or milk into a small bowl until smooth and consitent throughout batter.
  3. If using milk instead of water, can also add an egg or two for extra protein.
  4. Spray bottom of pan with a small amount of cooking spray or oil.
  5. Pour batter in desired size onto pan. Can be used to make a few large flapjacks or many smaller ones, according to preference.
  6. Wait until bubbles form on top side of flapjack and flip flapjack when bubbles stop forming/popping.
  7. Cook for a minute or so on opposite side (until cooked through).
  8. Repeat until batter has been used up.
  9. Serve with any number of toppings. Recommended: maple syrup, peanut butter, butter, small fruits/berries.
  10. Bon appetit!
